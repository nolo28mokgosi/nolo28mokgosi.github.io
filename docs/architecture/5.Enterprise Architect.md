In his blogpost, Steve argues that Enterprise Architecture is a business role, not a tech role.

He mentiones that when initiatives fails, bottleneck is rarely missing frameworks, but the big question of wether leaders understand the business they trying to change.

Given this Context, Enterprise Architects are expected to:
- Translate business needs into technology solutions
- Show how architecture improve revenue, reduces cost, manage risk, or accelerates time to market.
- Communicate clearly to the C-Suite
- Sell the architecture to executives, sterring committees, and the board.
